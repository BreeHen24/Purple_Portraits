Francis Tuttle Graphic Communications students work with the Child Development Center and the Pre-K classes to have a live-work opportunity for students and produce pictures.

Students work with a photographer, build a set, work with lightning and take of photos of the child-care and pre-k students. Then students use their Photoshop knowledge to be sure the photos are print ready. Parents have an opportunity to purchase the prints once editing is complete, students learn the ordering software and we order prints through an exceptional photography printer.

All proceeds go to our student organization fund, Business Professionals of America.

Thank you for supporting students!

Parent Experience:

Francis Tuttle students strive to give parents a high-quality service and product at a fraction of the cost leading school photography companies.

Our students strive to build a set that is appealing following current trends and take time and care with each subject, attempting to get the perfect photo, while watching intently to details to ensure a good photograph.
